How to Reach Srirangapatna

Srirangapatna is very close to Mysore, which is where all the important bus stations, rail connections and the airport is located. Mysore is 19 kms away and the historic town falls on the busy Bangalore to Mysore route, which makes it a very convenient destination to visit.

By Rail

A lot of trains from Bangalore that pass through Mysore also stop at Srirangapatnaâ€™s railway station. Early trains give you more time to explore. The nearest major railway station is in Mysore.

By Air

The closest airport is the Mysore airport which is only 16 kms away. You can get a cab from the airport to Mysore where you have many local transport options to Srirangapatna. The nearest international airport is in Bangalore, 165 kms away on the NH275.

By Road

Srirangapatna falls on the Bangalore-Mysore highway and you can hire a cab or drive yourself. You can also catch a bus from either of the two major cities.

The historic region of Mysore is one of the biggest cultural centres of Karnataka. Srirangapatna stands tall to this day, a reminder of some of the most crucial events that shaped the modern state. Blood and glory go side by side, as chronicled in the many tales of warrior kings across the history of humankind. A visit to Srirangapatna is the closest you will get to one such tale. Celebrate the Legacy of Tipu Sultan and immerse yourself in the history of this island town.
